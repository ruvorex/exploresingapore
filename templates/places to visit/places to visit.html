Revamped St James Power House

St James Power Station was the Municipal government's first foray into a power station of such large magnitude. Mapletree led two rounds of restorations in 2006 and 2018 to arrest the building's deterioration.

Berlayer Creek Boardwalk

Berlayer Creek is home to some 60 recorded bird species, 19 species of fishes and 14 true mangrove plant species. It also has one of the highest densities of molluscs in Singapore with some 50 species being recorded over a 5-hectare area.
