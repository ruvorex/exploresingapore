About Us

Junkie's Corner is established since 2003. The founders are collectors who have been collecting antique and vintage collectibles for more than 3 decades. Collections have piled up along the years and which they have decided to turn their passion into business to share with like-minded people. With a warehouse space of more than 18,000 sqft and thousands of collectibles items for you to shop around. You will be surprise to find items that are unbelievably special.
